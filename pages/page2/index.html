What is SMISKI?

Smiski are small, glow-in-the-dark figurines designed by the Japanese company Dreams Inc. Known for their quirky and often funny poses, Smiskis are typically found hiding in various corners of your home or workspace.

  • Derived from the Japanese phrase “隅にいる” (sumi ni iru) meaning "being in the corner"
  • Each SMISKI will come randomly in a blind box."
  • In each series comes 6 different poses + one SECRET SMISKI!

Where is SMISKI from?

Smiski was created by Dreams Inc., a Japanese company known for producing quirky and creative products. Dreams Inc. specializes in designing items that bring a sense of playfulness and charm into everyday life, and Smiski is one of their most successful lines. The creative team at Dreams Inc. aimed to create something that was not only collectible but also intriguing and mysterious.

  • Introduced on 2015
  • Japanese company Dreams Inc.
